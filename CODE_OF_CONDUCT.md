# Code of Conduct

## Expectations

Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate directly, respectfully, and with technical rigor.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- abusive or discriminatory language
- bad-faith review tactics
- publishing private data, credentials, or sensitive memory contents

## Project norms

- challenge ideas with evidence
- keep criticism specific to behavior, code, or product decisions
- mark assumptions clearly
- avoid overstating readiness or benchmark results

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior undermines productive collaboration or user safety.
